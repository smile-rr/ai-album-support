Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI photo selection work?

AI Album uses Apple's Vision framework to analyze photo quality, composition, and content locally on your device. No photos are uploaded to servers.

Is my data safe?

Yes, all AI processing happens locally on your device. Your photos never leave your device and are not uploaded to any cloud servers.

Why can't I see all my photos?

Please ensure you've granted photo access permission in Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos > AI Album.

How do I create a new album?

Tap the sparkles icon in the timeline view to access AI recommendations, then select photos and tap "Create Album".

App crashes or freezes

Try restarting the app. If the issue persists, please contact us with your device model and iOS version.

System Requirements

  • iOS 16.0 or later
  • iPhone or iPad
  • At least 100MB free storage

Privacy Policy

AI Album respects your privacy. All photo analysis is performed locally using Apple's Vision framework. We do not collect, store, or transmit your photos or personal data.
