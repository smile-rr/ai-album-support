AI Album - Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Data Collection

AI Album does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or photos. All photo analysis is performed locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework.

Photo Access

The app requests access to your photo library solely to display and organize your photos. Your photos never leave your device and are not uploaded to any servers.

Local Processing

All AI analysis, including photo quality assessment and content recognition, is performed entirely on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

AI Album does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@aialbum.app
